🔐 Crypto Wallet Security Tips for 2025

Date: May 10, 2025

As crypto grows in popularity, so do scams and hacking attempts. Protecting your wallet is more important than ever.

  • ✅ Use hardware wallets for cold storage
  • ✅ Enable 2FA on all crypto accounts
  • ✅ Never share your seed phrase
  • ✅ Use secure, updated apps only
