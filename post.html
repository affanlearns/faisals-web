Bitcoin Breaks Records in 2025

Published on June 25, 2025 • Category: Crypto

In a historic moment, Bitcoin surpassed $100,000 today, shattering previous market expectations and sending shockwaves through the financial industry.

Experts believe that institutional adoption and reduced supply are key drivers of this rally. Meanwhile, investors are now watching altcoins as Ethereum and Solana follow suit.

The future of digital currency appears stronger than ever, and many believe we are still early in the crypto revolution.
