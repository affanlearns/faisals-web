💡 Pakistan’s Rising Tech Boom

Date: June 12, 2025

Pakistan is emerging as a strong player in the global tech scene. With a young and talented population, freelance exports and local startups are seeing rapid growth.

From fintech apps to AI research, Pakistan’s developers are creating world-class products. Government support, remote work culture, and digital literacy programs are boosting this growth.
