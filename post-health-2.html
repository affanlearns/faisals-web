🧘 Mental Health in the Digital Era

Date: June 3, 2025

In a world full of screens and constant pings, mental health has never been more important. Digital fatigue, anxiety, and overthinking are now everyday issues.

Mindfulness apps, scheduled screen breaks, and digital detox weekends are powerful ways to restore mental clarity. Experts suggest balancing screen time with real-life interactions and rest.

Faisal Lab brings you mental wellness strategies to help you thrive — both online and offline — in the modern world.
